## Overview

The Cost Dashboard is the main view in Cost Optix. It aggregates billing data from all your connected cloud accounts and surfaces it as structured, actionable information — without requiring you to dig through provider portals or export CSVs.

The dashboard is account-scoped: use the account switcher in the top navigation to switch between connected accounts. All panels, charts, and metrics update to reflect the selected account and date range.

---

## Date Range

Every section of the dashboard is driven by the selected date range. Click **Change Dates** in the top-right to open the date picker.

Available presets include:

- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days
- This month / Last month
- Last 3 months / Last 6 months
- Custom range

The header shows both the formatted date range and the human-readable duration (e.g. `6 months`, `2 months + 1 partial`). Partial months — where the range starts or ends mid-month — are flagged so you know the figures aren't full-month totals.

<Info>
  Cloud providers have billing lag: today's spend may not appear for 24–72 hours depending on the provider. This is a provider constraint, not a Cost Optix limitation.
</Info>

## Executive Summary

The Executive Summary is a KPI panel at the top of the dashboard. It shows the health of the current billing month at a glance.

| Metric | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Month-to-Date Spend** | Total amortised cost for the current billing month so far |
| **vs Last Month** | Dollar and percentage change versus last month's final total |
| **Month-End Forecast** | Projected total cost if current patterns hold |
| **Cost Ceiling (95%)** | Risk-adjusted worst-case month-end using Value at Risk modelling |
| **Projected at Current Rate** | Linear burn-rate extrapolation for the remaining days |
| **Services Health** | Count of Critical / Watch / Healthy services |
| **Budget Utilisation** | % of budget consumed (shown when a budget is configured) |

### Amortised Cost

Cost Optix uses amortised cost by default. Upfront reservation and commitment payments are spread evenly across the commitment period rather than recorded as a spike on the purchase date. This keeps month-over-month comparisons meaningful and prevents false anomalies on reservation purchase days.

### Forecast Methods

The dashboard selects the most accurate forecast method available:

- **Risk-Adjusted (VaR 95%)** — used when sufficient historical data exists. Shows both a worst-case cost ceiling and a linear projection so you can see the range of likely outcomes.
- **Burn-Rate Projection** — used when historical data is limited. Calculates daily spend so far and extrapolates to month end.
- **API Forecast** — used for providers that supply native forecast data.

## Service Cost Trends

A line chart showing individual service costs over time. Services are plotted as separate lines, making it easy to see which ones are growing, stable, or declining.

- Toggle individual services on or off using the checkbox controls
- Use **Select All** / **Clear All** to manage visibility
- Click **View Details** to open the full-screen chart modal
- Click **Explore Costs** to navigate to the detailed cost breakdown page

Up to 15 services are shown in the toggle controls. All services appear in the chart when "Select All" is active.

---

## Service Health

Services are automatically categorised based on their month-over-month cost change and anomaly status:

| Category | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Critical** | Anomaly detected, or cost spike above the critical threshold (default: >50%) |
| **Watch** | Significant cost change above the watch threshold (default: >20%), or anomaly with declining cost |
| **Savings** | Cost reduction below the savings threshold (default: <-10%) |
| **Healthy** | Normal cost behaviour — stable or minor expected changes |

Thresholds are configurable via the **⚙️ Configure** button in the Executive Summary.

Each service card shows:
- Service name and monthly cost
- Month-over-month change with direction indicator
- Health reason (e.g. "Anomaly detected with 85% cost spike")
- **View resources** button to drill into individual resources for that service

### Resource Drill-Down

Click **View resources** on any service to open the resource-level breakdown. This shows the individual resources driving the service cost, their month-over-month change, and resource metadata (resource group, location, type).

## Actionable Insights

The dashboard generates up to five prioritised insights based on current spending patterns. Insights are triggered by conditions such as:

- **High burn rate** — projected month-end is significantly above expected
- **Rapid service growth** — a service is up more than 40% month-over-month
- **Potential savings** — services showing declining usage or significant cost reductions
- **Multiple anomalies** — three or more services with detected anomalies
- **Spend concentration** — a single service represents more than 30% of total spend

Each insight includes a cost impact estimate and a direct action button linking to the relevant section of the platform.

---

## Monthly Resource Comparison

The resource comparison view shows which individual resources increased, decreased, appeared as new, or were deleted between two billing months.

For each resource:
- Current month cost and previous month cost
- Absolute change in dollars
- Percentage change
- Share of total month-over-month delta
- Days active in each period
- Resource metadata (group, location, type)

Results are paginated and can be filtered by category: **All**, **Increases**, **Decreases**, **New**, or **Deleted**. Sorting is available by absolute change, percentage change, current cost, or resource name.

---

## Provider News

The dashboard surfaces relevant billing and service announcements from your connected cloud providers. Announcements are shown inline and can be dismissed individually.

---

## Anomaly Indicators

Detected anomalies are surfaced directly in the dashboard as service-level flags. An anomaly indicator on a service card means the service's spend deviated significantly from its statistical baseline on at least one day in the selected range.

Click the anomaly indicator to navigate to the Anomaly Detection page for full details, including severity, deviation percentage, and workflow status.

See [Anomaly Detection](/features/anomaly-detection) for how detection works and how to manage anomaly workflow states.

---

## Refreshing Data

Click **Refresh** in the top-right to pull the latest data for the selected account and date range. The account name and provider are shown in the account status bar during and after load.

Cost data is cached on first load. The refresh button bypasses the cache and fetches directly from the underlying data store.
