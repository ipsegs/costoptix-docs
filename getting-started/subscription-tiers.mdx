| Tier | Hosting |
|---|---|
| Starter | Shared managed infrastructure |
| Professional | Shared managed infrastructure |
| Business | Dedicated managed infrastructure |
| Enterprise | Self-hosted on your own VM or cloud environment |

Starter and Professional are logically isolated tenants on Cost Optix managed infrastructure. Business provides dedicated resources for stronger performance and compliance isolation. Enterprise is fully deployed within your own environment — on-premise or your preferred cloud provider.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

**What happens if I exceed my account or user limits?**
Cost Optix will prompt you to upgrade before additional accounts or users can be added. Existing accounts and users are never removed when limits are hit.

**Is there a free trial for paid tiers?**
Yes — new organisations receive a trial period on sign-up. Contact [support@costoptix.com](mailto:support@costoptix.com) for trial extension requests.

**Can Starter and Professional customers get SSO?**
SSO requires Business or Enterprise. It is not available on shared infrastructure tiers.
