## Starter — Free

The Starter tier is designed for individuals and small teams who want visibility into their cloud costs without commitment.

**Best for:** Solo developers, side projects, early-stage startups

**Includes:**
- 2 cloud accounts
- 1 user
- 30 days of historical data
- Up to 3 anomalies tracked per month
- 5 AI insights per month
- 100 API calls per day
- Webhook notifications

**Infrastructure:** Starter organizations run on a shared CloudCluster VM alongside Professional tier customers.

---

## Professional — $49/month

Professional adds multi-user support, more cloud accounts, extended historical data, and webhook integrations for growing teams.

**Best for:** Startups and small engineering teams actively managing cloud spend

**Includes everything in Starter, plus:**
- 5 cloud accounts
- 10 users
- 90 days of historical data
- Up to 100 anomalies per month
- 10 AI insights per month
- 1,000 API calls per day
- Budget forecasting

**Cloud spend fee:** 0.2% of monthly cloud spend above $10,000 (first $10K is always free)

**Infrastructure:** Professional organizations share the same CloudCluster VM as Starter tier.

<Note>
  **Example billing:** If your connected cloud spend is $15,000/month, the spend fee is 0.2% × $5,000 = $10. Total: $49 + $10 = $59/month.
</Note>

## Business — $199/month

Business gives mid-size teams a dedicated VM, SSO, advanced AI, and significantly higher limits across the board.

**Best for:** Mid-size companies and engineering teams with significant cloud footprint

**Includes everything in Professional, plus:**
- 50 cloud accounts
- 50 users
- 12 months of historical data
- Unlimited anomaly detection
- 100 AI insights per month
- 10,000 API calls per day
- **SSO integration** (SAML/OIDC)
- **Advanced AI models**
- Priority support

**Cloud spend fee:** 0.15% of monthly cloud spend above $25,000

**Infrastructure:** Business organizations run on a **dedicated CloudCluster VM** — compute resources are not shared with other customers.

<Warning>
  SSO is available on **Business and Enterprise tiers only**. If your organization requires SSO for compliance or access control, you will need at minimum the Business plan.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

**How is cloud spend calculated?**
Cloud spend is the total monthly cost reported by your connected cloud provider accounts. It excludes credits, refunds, and taxes, and only counts spend from accounts actively connected in Cost Optix.

**What happens if I exceed my account or user limits?**
Cost Optix will prompt you to upgrade before allowing additional accounts or users to be added. Existing accounts and users are never removed when limits are hit.

**Is there a free trial for paid tiers?**
Yes — new organizations receive a trial period when signing up. Reach out to [support@costoptix.com](mailto:support@costoptix.com) for trial extension requests.

**Can Starter and Professional customers get SSO?**
SSO is available on Business and Enterprise tiers only. This is because SSO requires dedicated infrastructure to maintain isolated identity provider sessions per organization.
