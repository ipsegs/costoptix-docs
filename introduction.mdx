## What is Cost Optix?

Cost Optix helps engineering teams, finance teams, and executives understand and control cloud spending across every provider in one place — without the complexity of enterprise tools or the limitations of single-provider dashboards.

## Who is Cost Optix for?

- **Engineering teams** who need resource-level cost attribution to understand which services or workloads are driving spend
- **Finance teams** who need budget tracking, forecasting, and audit-ready cost reports
- **Executives** who need high-level cloud portfolio health and spend trends
